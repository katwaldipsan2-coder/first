Business is an activity of buying and selling goods or services to earn profit. It plays an important role in the economy by creating jobs and helping people meet their daily needs. There are many types of businesses, such as small businesses, online businesses, and large companies. A successful business needs planning, hard work, and good customer service. In today’s world, online business is growing very fast because of the internet. People can sell products and services through websites and apps. In conclusion, business is important for personal income and the development of a country.
